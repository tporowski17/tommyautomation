About Tommy Automation

Born from a Real-World Need

Tommy Automation started with a single, practical challenge: how to bridge the gap between a busy IT helpdesk and critical device management tools. Our first project was born out of this necessity—an application to integrate Microsoft Intune device management directly into Zendesk for a university's IT department.

This initial success solidified our mission: to create targeted, high-impact automations that solve the real-world problems faced by IT professionals. We specialize in the Microsoft ecosystem, focusing on Azure and IT general automations to streamline complex processes and free up your team for more strategic work.

Featured Project

Case Study: Zendesk & Intune Integration

Bringing Device Management to the Helpdesk

For a busy university IT department, switching between Zendesk tickets and the Intune portal to look up device information was a slow, multi-step process. This created delays in support and increased the chance of human error.

We developed a custom Zendesk app that allows technicians to view and perform basic Intune device management actions—like sync, restart, or check compliance—directly from the ticket interface, saving valuable time on every support request.
